Wind Speed by City Latitude

This analysis examined each city's wind speed with respect to it's latitude. The plot shows the variance in wind speeds remains consistent across latitudes with a steady average around 5 mph. There are a few cities that we classify as outliers, with wind speeds above 25 mph. For all of these cases the cities are located at latitudes around 60 degrees North; however there are too few to be interpreted as a trend.
